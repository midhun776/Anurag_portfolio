About

I am a multi-instrumental session artist, singer-songwriter, and music producer who has been an integral part of multiple prestigious projects (artistic/cultural journeys I would say). These experiences have been pivotal in exploring various facets of myself as an artist.

Over the past five years, my immersion in the Indian film industry has allowed me to craft a unique musical identity, reflecting a passion for innovation and storytelling, each story a unique sonic collage. Eager to contribute this rich tapestry of experiences to any musical endeavor, I look forward to the next chapter of my artistic journey.

In addition to my artistic journey, I bring a solid foundation in academic qualifications.I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications (BCA) with proficiency in Java and C++. This technical background complements my creative pursuits, allowing me to seamlessly navigate the intersection of technology and music. Furthermore, my proficiency in music production software, including Logic Pro X and Protools, underscores my commitment to staying at the forefront of technological advancements in the field. This blend of artistic passion and technical acumen positions me as a well-rounded and adaptable contributor to any creative project.
